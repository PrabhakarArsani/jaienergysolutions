Government Subsidy Information

Avail maximum benefits from PM Surya Ghar Yojana and state subsidies

PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana

The PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana is a flagship central government scheme aimed at promoting rooftop solar installations across India. Under this scheme, households can receive substantial subsidies to install solar panels on their rooftops.

Up to ₹78,000

Central Government Subsidy on Rooftop Solar

The scheme targets installation of 1 crore rooftop solar systems, enabling households to generate their own electricity and reduce dependence on the grid.

Subsidy Structure

Subsidy Benefits

1-2 kW Systems

Central subsidy of ₹30,000 for rooftop solar systems of 1-2 kW capacity. Ideal for small homes with moderate electricity consumption.

3 kW and Above

Central subsidy of ₹78,000 for systems of 3 kW and above. Best value for homes with higher energy needs.

Free Electricity

Generate up to 300 units of free electricity per month, significantly reducing or eliminating your electricity bills.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Indian citizen with a valid electricity connection
  • Own house with suitable rooftop space
  • Residential electricity consumer (domestic tariff)
  • No previous central subsidy availed for rooftop solar
  • Valid identity proof and electricity bill
  • Rooftop area sufficient for desired system size

Required Documents

  • Recent electricity bill (last 3 months)
  • Property ownership proof or NOC from owner
  • Bank account details for subsidy transfer
  • Passport size photograph
