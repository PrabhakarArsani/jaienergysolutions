Residential Solar Solutions

Power your home with clean solar energy and slash your electricity bills

Rooftop Solar for Homes

Transform your home into a mini power plant with our residential rooftop solar solutions. Designed specifically for homes in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, our systems harness the region's abundant sunshine to generate clean electricity right on your rooftop.

Whether you have a small independent house or a large villa, we design custom solar systems that match your energy needs and roof space. Our residential solutions are perfect for reducing dependence on the grid and protecting your family from rising electricity costs.

System Types

Choose Your Solar System

🔌

On-Grid Systems

Connected to the electricity grid. Export excess power and earn credits through net metering. Most cost-effective option with government subsidy support.

🔋

Off-Grid Systems

Completely independent from the grid with battery storage. Ideal for remote areas or locations with unreliable grid power.

⚡

Hybrid Systems

Best of both worlds—grid-connected with battery backup. Enjoy net metering benefits plus power during outages.
