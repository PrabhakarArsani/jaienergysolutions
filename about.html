Founder & CEO

Er. Varun Kumar Sunnapu, the Founder of Jai Energy Solutions.

Varun is an Electrical Engineer with an M.Tech degree and has over 11 years of experience in the solar industry. After working in various technical and leadership roles, he started Jai Energy Solutions in 2022 with a vision to make solar energy affordable and accessible for everyone.

Today, Jai Energy Solutions has expanded to five branches across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana and has successfully completed more than 1,000 rooftop solar installations under the PM Surya Ghar Scheme.

Known for his technical knowledge, commitment, and customer-first approach, he continues to help families and businesses switch to clean energy.

Jai Energy solutions (JES) is the Pioneer in solar energy services and was started in 2020, Founded by Engineer.Sunnapu varun kumar who pursued his B.Tech Electrical engineering from JNTU kakinada and M.Tech nano technology from JNTU Hyderabad.

Over the last decade Engineer.sunnapu varun kumar has experienced exponential growth as technical engineer, technical manager and projects head in different reputed solar energy companies and now he is the founder of Jai Energy Solutions..

JES has delivered over 1000+ happy customers in the last 3 years ..it has installed around 3 MW rooftop and ground mounted solar systems including domestic, commercial and industrial projects.

JES is doing operation across India with branches at Telangana(hyd), Andhra Pradesh( tirupati )

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become the most trusted solar energy partner in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, leading the transition to sustainable energy for every household and business.

Our Mission

  • Deliver high-quality solar installations with exceptional customer service
  • Make solar energy affordable through subsidies and financing options
  • Create employment opportunities through franchise and partner programs
  • Contribute to India's renewable energy goals and environmental sustainability

Commitment to Clean Energy

At Jai Energy Solutions, clean energy is more than a business—it's our responsibility. Every solar panel we install contributes to reducing carbon emissions and combating climate change.

We are committed to using only environmentally responsible practices in our operations, from sourcing sustainable materials to ensuring proper recycling of old equipment. Our goal is to help Andhra Pradesh and Telangana achieve their renewable energy targets while creating a greener future for generations to come.
