FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about solar energy and our services.

How much can I save with rooftop solar?
With a properly sized solar system, you can reduce your electricity bill by up to 90%. A typical 3kW system can save ₹15,000-20,000 annually depending on your consumption and tariff rates.
What is the PM Surya Ghar Yojana subsidy?
PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana offers central government subsidy of up to ₹78,000 for rooftop solar installations. For 1-2 kW systems, subsidy is ₹30,000, and for 3 kW and above, it's ₹78,000.
How long does installation take?
Typical residential installation takes 3-5 days after site survey. Commercial and industrial projects may take 2-4 weeks depending on system size. Net metering approval usually takes 15-30 days.
Do solar panels work during power cuts?
On-grid systems shut down during power cuts for safety. However, hybrid systems with battery backup can provide power during outages. We offer both on-grid and hybrid solutions.
What warranty do you provide?
We provide 30-year performance warranty on solar panels, 5-10 year warranty on inverters, and 5-year workmanship warranty on installation. Extended warranties are available on select products.
